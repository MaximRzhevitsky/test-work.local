Why We Do This

Our center offers a welcoming and inclusive environment for individuals of all faiths and beliefs. Whether you are new to spirituality or a seasoned practitioner, we offer a variety of programs and services designed to support you on your journey.

Whether you're a longtime member of our church or someone who's curious about what we do, we hope that this video will give you a sense of who we are and what we stand for. We hope to see you soon!

Our Core Values

Love and Compassion

A commitment to showing love and compassion to all members of the community, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Faith and Spirituality

A focus on faith and spirituality as a means of personal growth, self-discovery, and building a strong relationship with God.

Service and Outreach

A dedication to serving the needs of the local and global community through volunteerism, charitable giving to promote social justice and equality.
